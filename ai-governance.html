AI Governance

My recent work focuses on Community led AI Governance. This is accomplished with the use of Constitutions that provide the guardrails (things not to do) and behavioral guides (things to do) for underlying AI systems. The important aspect is that Consitutions are owned by the stakeholders, can be changed by the stackholders, and provide feedback on an AI's operation.
